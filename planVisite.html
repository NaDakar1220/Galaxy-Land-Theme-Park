Can I bring my own food?

Outside food is not allowed, but exceptions are made for medical or allergy-related needs.

Is there a discount for groups or schools?

Yes! Please contact our group sales team at groups@galaxyland.com.

Is Galaxy Land wheelchair accessible?

Yes, most attractions are accessible and rentals are available near the entrance.

Are pets allowed in the park?

No pets are allowed, except service animals with appropriate identification.
